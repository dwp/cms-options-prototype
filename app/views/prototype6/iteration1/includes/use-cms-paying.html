The Child Maintenance Service may be able to arrange child maintenance for you. Using the Child Maintenance Service is less flexible than making your own arrangement because the calculation is a fixed amount based on the paying parent’s earnings.

Using the Child Maintenance Service might be a good option if:

  • you do not have contact with the other parent
  • you do not feel safe dealing with the other parent
  • you do not think the other parent will stick to an agreement
  • work out how much child maintenance you will pay
  • arrange for the you to pay the other parent
  • take action if you do not make payments
  • enforce or take legal action to recover any missed maintenance payments.
  • help find the other parent if you’ve lost contact
  • recalculate the payment amount if a changes in your circumstances is reported. For example, if your salary changes
  • help if someone says they are not the parent of a child

It will take about 6 weeks to process an application and to begin processing payments. The receiving parent should allow 10-12 weeks from the application date to receive their first payment. If contact cannot be made with the other parent, the application will take longer.

How is the payment calculation made?
We take into account your income (both employment and benefits) and any other children you are responsible for.

We do not take into account the finances or other children of the other parent.
