The Child Maintenance Service can:

  • work out how much child maintenance you should get and when you should get it 
  • take action if payments are not made in full or on time
  • help find the other parent if you have lost contact
  • help if the other parent denies they are the child's biological or legal parent 

How you get child maintenance

There are two child maintenance service types:

Direct pay - the other parent will pay child maintenance into your bank or building society. You manage payments using an online account. 

Collect and Pay - the Child Maintenance Service will take payment from the other parent and pass it on to you. There are fees for both parents if you use this service.
