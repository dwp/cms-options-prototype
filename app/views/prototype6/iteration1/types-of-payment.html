If you are worried about your safety

You do not need to have any contact with the other parent to use the Child Maintenance Service.

The Child Maintenance Service will send you text messages

If you do not think it is safe for you to get text messages about child maintenance, you should apply by phone.

Contact the Child Maintenance Service

Sharing your bank or building society details with the other parent

The Child Maintenance Service may share your bank or building society details with the other parent.  if you do not want the other parent to know your location, you can set up a non-geographical bank account.

You will need to contact your bank or building society to set this up. If you need a non-geographical bank account, you should set it up before you start an application.
