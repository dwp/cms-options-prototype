Am I eligible to receive Child Benefit?

If I receive Universal credit:
Yes. Child Benefit and Universal Credit are different services - the child element of Universal Credit is not Child Benefit, and you do not need to receive Universal Credit to receive Child Benefit.

If I have more than two children:
Yes. There is no limit to how many children you can claim Child Benefit payments for.

If I'm not a UK national:
You may be able to get Child Benefit if you have moved to the UK. If your visa says "no recourse to public funds" - do not apply for Child Benefit. You will be unsuccessful and this will slow your Child Maintenance application

How do I apply to receive Child Benefit for my child?

You can find out how to apply for Child Benefit online (link opens in a new tab).
