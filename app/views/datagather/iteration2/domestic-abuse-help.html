Get help if you or your children have experienced domestic abuse

We're asking you this so we can show you where to get support if you need it. We will not share your answers with the other parent or anyone else.

You may have been in an abusive relationship if you or your children have experienced any of the following behaviours:

  • Psychological or emotional abuse
  • Physical or sexual abuse
  • Financial or economic abuse
  • Coercive control (an act or pattern of acts of assault, threats, humiliation, intimidation, degradation, isolation and control)
  • Harassment and stalking
  • Online or digital abuse
