Tell us about children who may affect your calculation

Children must be under 16 or under 20 if they are in full- time education or training, up to and including A level or equivalent. See HMRC for the list of education and approved training (opens in new tab).

To calculate your child maintenance, we need to ask you:

  • about the children named in the application
  • if you pay child maintenance for children in a private arrangement (not using the Child Maintenance Service)
  • if any children live with you (for example, your child or a partner’s child)

If you do not think you are the parent of a child named in this application

We need to collect your information so we can decide if you have legal responsibility for the child. We usually need evidence if you do not think you're the parent (opens in new tab).
