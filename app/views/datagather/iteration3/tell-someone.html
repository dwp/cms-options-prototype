Tell someone about the behaviours you or your children have experienced

You may have been in an abusive relationship. Domestic abuse or violence is a crime. It is important to remember that there is help and support available to you, including police response, online support, refuges and other services.

! Warning Call 999 if it’s an emergency or you’re in immediate danger.
