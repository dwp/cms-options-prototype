What happens next

Once we have reviewed your information, we will send you a text message and a letter to confirm how much child maintenance you need to pay and when you need to pay it. 

Find out how much you need to pay

We cannot confirm how much you will need to pay until we have reviewed your information. This usually takes 6 weeks or less. It can take longer if there are disputes about the information we have been given. 

You can enter your details into the Child Maintenance Calculator (opens in new tab) to get an estimated payment amount. Your actual child maintenance payments may be different to this estimation. 

Managing your child maintenance

Sign in to your online child maintenance account using your:

  • Customer reference number - you can find this on letters from the Child Maintenance Service, it starts with 121
  • National Insurance Number
  • 7-digit personal identification number (PIN) – you can reset your PIN if you cannot remember it
